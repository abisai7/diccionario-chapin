### Etymology

In Guatemalan Spanish, _seco_ literally means "dry," but it is commonly used as a colloquial nickname or adjective to describe someone who is very thin or lean. The term is often used informally among friends and family and is usually not intended as an insult.

### Usage

_Seco_ is used to refer to a person with a very slim or skinny build. It can be used as either a descriptive adjective or a nickname, especially in casual conversations.
