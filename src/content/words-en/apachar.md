word: Apachar

category: verb
examples:
  - Apachá the button to turn it on.
  - I need to apachar the dough so it becomes thinner.
  - He/She apachó the can so it would fit in the bin.
region: Guatemala
synonyms:
  - press
  - push
  - crush
