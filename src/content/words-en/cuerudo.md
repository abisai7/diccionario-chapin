  - "He's very cuerudo, he doesn't care what people say about him."
  - "How cuerudo you are, you're not ashamed of anything."
  - "You have to be cuerudo to endure so much criticism."

## Usage

Guatemalan term used to describe a person who has the ability to withstand criticism without being fazed, or who acts without shame in situations that would normally cause embarrassment.
