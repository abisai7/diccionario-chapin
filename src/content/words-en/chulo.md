---
word: "Chulo"
meaning: "Handsome, pretty, or attractive; pleasing to look at."
examples:
  - "Your curly dog is so chulo."
  - "That girl I met in Huehue was really chula."
  - "Your room looks chulo after painting it."
category: "adjective"
region: "Guatemala"
synonyms: ["cute", "pretty", "nice"]
relatedWords: ["chilero", "nitido"]
---

## Usage

Very common in Guatemalan speech to describe something or someone that is attractive, pretty, or pleasant. Used for both people and objects.
